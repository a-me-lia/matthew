# Living Monotonously

Throughout the entirety of the critically acclaimed 2023 film "Suzume No Tojimari" (すずめの戸締まり), director <Inline href='http://en.shinkaiworks.com/'>Makoto Shinkai,</Inline> continues with one of his favorite motifs in his career, carefully
sprinkling reminders of the constant everyday lives happeneing around the events of the film.

<Link href='https://suzume-movie.com'>Suzume no Tojimari official website</Link>

## Selling emotion with music

<Inline href='https://radwimps.jp/en/'>RADWIMPS,</Inline>
the band known for their longtime collaboration with Shinkai films really just cannot disappoint. The centerpiece track, "Suzume", strays far from their usual synth-and-orchestral style of composition used for centerpieces such as Weathering with You's
<Inline href='https://youtu.be/saDmN2f3HI0?t=255'>Grand Escape</Inline>. One of the most notable differences is the usage of traditional instruments, specifically chosen to serve as an echo of the central topic of abandoned places and the unceasing moving on of our everyday lives.
However, the simple and playful piano melodies omnipresent through Shinkai's recent films is still in the same warm, familiar 
slice-of-life scenes. The music of Suzume no Tojimari is truely great in that...
